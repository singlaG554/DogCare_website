Our Services

Pet Feeding

Our daycare offers a variety of feeding options including dry or wet food , homemade meals, or special diets for pets with specific health needs. Some facilities may also offers treats or snacks throughout the day. We follow the feeding instructions provided by the pet owner to ensure that the pets are receiving the appropriate type and amount of food. Our staff monitor the pets during feeding to ensure that they are eating and drinking properly. There are also facilities for handling food allergies or other dietary restrictions, and also proper storage and handling procedures for food to ensure that it is fresh and safe for the pets. Overall, we ensure that pets are properly fed and cared for while their owners are away. By providing appropriate food and monitoring feeding, these facilities help to promote the health and well-being of our animal companions.

Hommies for Doggies

We offer boarding facilities for your pets while keeping them healthy and happy. They are manned by staff who have expertise in handling pets like dogs and cats. We provide both Air Conditioned and non-Air Conditioned accommodations. They are also lots of toys for pets to keep them engaged. Our team prepare home-cooked meals for the pets. Medical attention is available to them whenever the need arises.
